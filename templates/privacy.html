Privacy Policy

Last updated March 2026

Data We Collect

We collect the account details you provide when registering — your name, email address, and password — along with the expenses you record, including amounts in rupees, categories, dates, and descriptions.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate Spendly: to authenticate your account and to display your expenses, summaries, and spending patterns back to you. We do not use your financial information for advertising or sell it to anyone.

Data Storage

Your information is stored in our application database. Passwords are kept in a hashed form and are never stored as plain text. You may delete your expenses or your entire account at any time.

Third Party Services

Spendly loads fonts from Google Fonts to render the interface. We do not share your account details or expense data with any third-party advertisers or analytics providers.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
